Music

Music is how my family and friends stay connected. Sharing our favorite songs or blasting music in the car together helps us form closer bonds. Due to COVID-19, I could not see many concerts I had planned to visit during the summer I graduated. I am glad to say I have been to four shows in the last six months and have enjoyed every minute. My friends and I follow a saying that if tickets are under $100 for a well-known (to us) artist, then we are going, no question.
