Fashion

I love clothes. Is that strange, maybe, but I feel you could do anything if you have the right outfit. Such as, if I need to get a job, I dress the part, whatever that may entail, a suit? I’ll wear a suit, and if you don’t own one, buy one because, let me tell you, in a power suit, I believe I can conquer the world (the outfit will not help with execution, but you will look fly. Clothes give people power. Clothes provide the power of confidence and the ability to express themselves by simply choosing what to wear that day. You get to decide how people see you.
